The Maple House Community

Christians joining the mission of God in Searcy, AR. Join us in spreading the Kingdom of God to our neighborhood through relationships, tutoring, and community gardening.

Neighbors in the heart of Searcy, AR

The Maple House Community is a group of neighbors around S. Maple Street in Searcy, AR. We meet at the Maple House for games, worship, after-school tutoring, and gardening with the community.
